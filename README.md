A specialized Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform designed specifically for the nutraceutical gummies business. This system streamlines customer interactions, order tracking, and inventory management, allowing sales and support teams to scale efficiently.

## ✨ Features

* **Customer & Lead Management:** Track client profiles, purchase history, and communication logs.
* **Order Tracking Pipeline:** Visualize orders from checkout to delivery.
* **Product Inventory Integration:** Monitor stock levels for different SKUs (e.g., multivitamin, melatonin, ashwagandha gummies).
* **Analytics Dashboard:** Monitor sales metrics, customer retention rates, and best-performing product lines.
